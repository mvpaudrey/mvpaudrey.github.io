
  Welcome to Halftime Football support. This page provides help, FAQs, and contact details for the app.

  If you need assistance, email us at: contact@cheulah.com

  ## Features

  - Live match updates with key events
  - Full AFCON fixtures and results
  - Group standings and qualification tracking
  - Team and player profiles with stats
  - Push notifications for news and live score updates

  ## FAQ

  Q: Do I need an account to use the app?
  A: No. The app works without any login.

  Q: Are there any in‑app purchases or subscriptions?
  A: No. The app has no in‑app purchases.

  Q: Why am I not receiving notifications?
  A: Check that notifications are enabled for Halftime Football in iOS Settings. Also verify that Focus or Do Not Disturb isn’t blocking alerts.

  Q: How often are live scores updated?
  A: Scores and key events update in real time when a match is in progress.

  Q: The app shows outdated data. What can I do?
  A: Pull down to refresh, or close and reopen the app. If the issue persists, contact support.

  ## Troubleshooting

  Notifications not arriving

  - Go to iOS Settings > Notifications > Halftime Football and enable alerts.
  - Ensure Low Power Mode or Focus isn’t suppressing notifications.
  - Check your internet connection.

  App is slow or crashes

  - Make sure you’re on the latest iOS version.
  - Force quit and reopen the app.
  - If the issue continues, email support with your device model and iOS version.

  Scores or standings look incorrect

  - Pull down to refresh.
  - If it persists, contact support with the match or team name.
